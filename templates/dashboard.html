Chat Commands
!pet @user STREAMER
Streamer-only. Highlights a viewer in the center of the overlay with a heart animation & sound effect — use it to spotlight someone briefly.
!boop @user EVERYONE
Boops another viewer (shows their emoji).
!namecolor pink SUBS
Changes their username color. Accepts color names (pink, coral, hotpink...) or hex codes (#ff6600).
!emoji 👽 SUBS
Changes their personal emoji to any standard emoji.

Share Instructions

Paste this into your chat to teach viewers how to play:

Use !boop @user to interact! Subs can also use !emoji 👽 (any standard emoji) to change their buddy, and !namecolor pink (or !namecolor #ff6600) to change their name color. (!pet @user is streamer-only.)
🔌 INSTALLATION
Create a Browser Source in OBS and paste this URL:
https://turbotack.rustyblu.com/emojibuddy/overlay/{channel_id}
Recommended: 960 x 540 px
⚠️ Enable Audio: Check "Control Audio via OBS" in source properties to hear the squeak! 🔊
